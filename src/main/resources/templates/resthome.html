Rest End Points



Survey


Get all surveys: /surveys || example: "https://survey-task.herokuapp.com/surveys" || Type: GET

Get a survey by ID: /surveys/id || example: "https://survey-task.herokuapp.com/surveys/1" || Type: GET

Save new survey: /surveys || example: "https://survey-task.herokuapp.com/surveys/" || Type: POST


Question


Get all questions: /questions || example: "https://survey-task.herokuapp.com/questions" || Type: GET


Answer


Get all answers to all questions: /answers || example: "https://survey-task.herokuapp.com/answers" || Type: GET

Save new answer: /answers || example: "https://survey-task.herokuapp.com/answers" || Type: POST
